Life 2.0

This is a documentary from 2010 about Second Life. The director took 4 avatars and told their histories.

The first one it’s the avatar from a woman who make houses, objects, clothes… she makes 6 digits in a year. The second it’s a couple, she is been married about 11 years and he is been married about 20 years, they are “in love”. The third it’s a guy and he’s avatar is a 11 years old girl.
